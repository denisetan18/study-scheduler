Fixed schedule

Things that don't move — school, sleep, CCA, tuition, meals. Key everything in at once like a spreadsheet, then save to update your calendar. Fixed blocks always show up grey on the calendar, and you can drag them around too if something changes last-minute.

Variable tasks

One-off work with a deadline. Tell DayStack how many 30-min blocks it needs and how many sessions to split it into — it'll slot the sessions into your free time, soonest deadlines and top priority first. Mark something as Exam / Test and "Sessions" becomes chapter count: DayStack studies each chapter in full across the first ~70% of your runway, then schedules a short revision pass per chapter (about a quarter of the time) in the final 2–4 days before it.
